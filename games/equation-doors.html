📖 Controls

  • ↑ ↓ ← → or W A S D – Move
  • Shift (hold while moving) – Pull block behind you
  • E or 🧲 Pull button – Pull adjacent block toward you
  • H or 💡 Hint button – Show hint (up to 3 levels)
  • R or 🔄 Reset button – Restart current room
  • N or ▶ Next button – Go to next room (when unlocked)
